Task 5 : Prevention Checklist:

Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
2. Do not approve unknown payment requests
3. Always check the sender before clicking any link
4. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
5. Use strong passwords for all accounts
6. Enable two-factor authentication where possible
7. Install apps only from official app stores
8. Keep your phone and apps updated regularly
UPI/Financial Safety Tips:
9. Remember: you do NOT need to enter PIN to receive money
10. Always verify before scanning QR code.
Reporting : Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline Number: 1930
