##  Project Overview
This project is based on building basic digital skills and understanding how to use online tools in a proper and safe way. 
It includes tasks like creating designs, setting up professional profiles, practising coding, writing emails, and learning about cyber safety.
The main aim of this project is to improve digital awareness and prepare for academic and professional use of technology.

---

## Task 1 – Create a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
In this task, I created a digital awareness poster using Canva. 
The design focuses on safe internet usage and responsible online behaviour.
I learned how to use templates, colors, and layouts to make a simple and clear design.

---

## Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help in building a professional online presence.
I added my details, explored features, and understood how they are useful for learning and career growth.

---

## Task 3 – Platforms & Collaboration
I created an account on HackerRank and solved a beginner-level problem to practise coding.
I also created a Google Form for a digital awareness quiz and viewed responses using Google Sheets.
This helped me understand collaboration and data collection.

---

## Task 4 – Email & Communication
In this task, I wrote professional emails such as requesting an extension and applying for an internship.
I also created a checklist for responsible social media use. This task helped me improve my communication skills.

---

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
I studied cybercrime topics like UPI fraud and learned how such scams happen.
I also created a prevention checklist to stay safe online.
This task increased my awareness about online risks and safety measures.
